Frequently Asked Questions

What is FreelancerHub?

FreelancerHub is a platform that connects freelancers with clients looking to get work done remotely.

How do I post a project?

Simply sign in as a client and navigate to the Post Project page to fill out your project details.

How do freelancers get paid?

Freelancers get paid through the platform once the project is completed and approved by the client.

Can I hire freelancers from other countries?

Yes, FreelancerHub allows you to hire talent from anywhere in the world.

Is it free to join FreelancerHub?

Yes, both clients and freelancers can sign up for free.

How do I contact support?

You can contact our support team via the email or phone number listed in the footer.
